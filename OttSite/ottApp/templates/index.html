Otter Paradise

Welcome to the
Otter Paradise Sanctuary!

Welcome to the Otter Paradise Sanctuary located in rustic Nowhere, OK! Just half of a mile off the shore of Fort Cobb Lake. A small, friendly, psuedo-family run Caddo County attraction. Perfect for any quasi-family trip in any-weather!

Visitation

We pride ourselves on our avalibility! Our family works around the clock in order to make sure your visit is perfect! We open our gates to you at 10am and close at 5pm. Closed on major holidays.
We have our feeding times listed in the Otter Information page The feeding times would be an excellent time to visit.
